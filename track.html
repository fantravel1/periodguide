Why Track Daily?

Even 30 seconds of daily logging builds a powerful picture over time. After 2-3 cycles, PeriodGuide can detect patterns and correlations in your data that would be invisible otherwise.

  • ✓ Spot symptom patterns before they become problems
  • ✓ Discover sleep-mood-pain correlations
  • ✓ Prepare better for doctor appointments
  • ✓ Understand your unique cycle rhythm
  • ✓ All data stays on your device

What You Can Track

Six key dimensions that paint a complete picture of your daily wellbeing. All optional, all private.

Pain Level

Pain (0-10)

Track cramp intensity, headaches, and body pain on a simple 0-10 scale. See how it correlates with your cycle day.

Mood

Mood (0-10)

From low to high, capture your emotional state. Over time, you'll see which cycle days tend to affect mood most.

Energy

Energy (0-10)

Your energy fluctuates with hormones. Tracking it helps you plan demanding vs. restful days around your cycle.

Bleeding

Bleeding Level

None, light, medium, or heavy. Track flow patterns to understand your unique menstrual rhythm and detect changes.

Sleep

Sleep (hours)

Sleep quality changes across your cycle. Log your hours to discover correlations with mood, pain, and energy.

Stress

Stress (0-10)

Stress impacts your cycle. Tracking it alongside symptoms helps identify triggers and resilience patterns.
