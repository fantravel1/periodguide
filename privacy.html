Privacy Center

Your intimate health data belongs to you. Period.

We do not sell intimate data. Period.

PeriodGuide is built on trust. Your menstrual health data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for targeted ads. We believe privacy isn't a feature — it's architecture.

Choose Your Privacy Level

🔒
Default

Anonymous Mode

Your data is stored with a random anonymous key generated on your device. No email, no name, no personally identifying information ever leaves your browser. The server stores only hashed identifiers.

  • ✓ Random anonymous key
  • ✓ Syncs across sessions
  • ✓ Export & delete anytime
  • ✓ No account required
🛡️
Maximum

Local-Only Mode

Zero network calls. Everything stays in your browser's local storage. No data ever leaves your device. Perfect for maximum privacy. You can still export your data as a file.

  • ✓ Zero server communication
  • ✓ Browser-only storage
  • ✓ Local file export
  • ✓ Full guidance still works
👤
Optional

Account Mode

For cross-device convenience only. Even with an account, we collect minimal data, never share it, and you can delete everything at any time. Accounts are never required.

  • ✓ Cross-device sync
  • ✓ Same privacy guarantees
  • ✓ Export & delete anytime
  • ✓ Never required
