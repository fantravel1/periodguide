Questions to Ask

Check the questions you want to remember for your appointment. You can print this page to bring with you.

About Your Cycle

Is my cycle length normal for my age?

Share your average cycle length from your tracking data.

Should I be concerned about my bleeding pattern?

Mention duration, heaviness, and any changes you've noticed.

What could be causing my irregular cycles?

Relevant if your cycles vary significantly in length.

About Pain

Is my pain level normal, or should we investigate further?

Share your average and maximum pain scores from tracking.

Could my symptoms indicate endometriosis or another condition?

Especially if pain is severe, extends beyond your period, or affects daily activities.

What pain management options should I consider?

Ask about both medication and non-medication approaches.

About PMS/Mood

Could my mood symptoms indicate PMDD rather than PMS?

PMDD is a clinical condition that responds well to treatment. Share your mood tracking data.

Are there specific supplements that could help with my symptoms?

Ask about evidence-based options like magnesium, calcium, vitamin B6.

General

Should I have any blood work done?

Iron levels, thyroid function, and hormone panels may be relevant.

When should I follow up or come back?

Get a clear timeline for reassessment.

Tips for Your Visit

📝 Before Your Visit

  • Export your cycle tracking data
  • Check the questions you want to ask
  • Note your top 3 concerns
  • Write down current medications/supplements
  • Print this page to bring along

💬 During Your Visit

  • Share your tracking data summary
  • Describe symptoms in specific terms
  • Ask your prepared questions
  • Take notes on recommendations
  • Ask for written aftercare instructions
